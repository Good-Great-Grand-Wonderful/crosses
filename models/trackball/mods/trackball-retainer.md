# Trackball Retainer

## Description
A trackball retainer mod for the Crosses/Bridges keyboards.

## Compatibility
- **Status:** Only tested with the **Bridges** variant.

## Bill of Materials (BOM)
- **Bearings:** 6x 2.8mm diameter bearings.
  - *Note:* 3mm bearings may work but have not been tested.
- **Heat Inserts:** 
  - Hole requires a **4.4mm** diameter heat insert (slightly larger than the standard ones used for Crosses/Bridges).
